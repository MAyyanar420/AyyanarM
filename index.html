Adhi College of Engineering and Technology (ACET) is a private engineering college in Oragadam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu:

Established: 2008 Affiliation: Anna University, Chennai Approval: All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and the Government of Tamil Nadu Courses: BTech, BE, MTech, ME, and PhD programs in various specializations Admission: Entrance-based, with BTech admissions based on TNEA scores Colors: Blue and gold

B.TECH COURSES

  • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science

Admissions are based on merit and entrance exams, such as CEETA PG.
